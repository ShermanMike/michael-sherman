Understanding the Stock Market

What is the Stock Market?

The stock market is where shares of publicly traded companies are bought and sold. As Warren Buffett often says, it's a place where ownership in businesses changes hands. The term stock exchange refers to the physical or virtual marketplace where these transactions occur.

"The stock market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient."
- Warren Buffett

Key Market Indices

Major market indices include:

  • S&P 500 - Tracks 500 large US companies
  • DJIA - Follows 30 significant stocks
  • NASDAQ - Technology-heavy index
  • The Nikkei 225 Nikkei Stock Average - Japanese market index
